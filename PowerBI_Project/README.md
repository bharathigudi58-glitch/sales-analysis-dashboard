# Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project was developed using Power BI to analyze sales data from the Superstore dataset.

## Tools Used

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* DAX

## KPIs Created

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Total States

## Visualizations

* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Sales by Segment
* Top 10 States by Sales
* Top 10 Customers by Sales
* Sales by Ship Mode

## Objective

To analyze sales performance and provide insights into customer, regional, and product sales trends.

## Key Insights

* Sales performance varies across regions.
* Customer segments contribute differently to total sales.
* Some states generate significantly higher sales than others.
* Product categories show different sales patterns.
